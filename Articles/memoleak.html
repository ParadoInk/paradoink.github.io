BREAKING: Internal SRA Leak, Should Cold_Brew_Coffee resign?

By
Parada

HSCTiger09 calls for SRA Head Cold_Brew_Coffee to resign.

At Bolshevik Magazine, we typically decide to not report breaking news, instead covering things with the benefit of hindsight and extra thought. Today is an exception, however, as we have received a leaked memo from the internal leadership of the Socialist Rifle Association. Although not directly linked to the Socialist Party, and not necessarily always Socialist, as Democratic Presidential Candidate Plebit has shown us, but it does share many close associations, including its leaders: specifically former Socialist Chief /u/Cold_Brew_Coffee.

I have been extremely critical of SRA Chair Coffee, and I have also been critical of candidate HSCTiger09 for associating himself with Coffee and the SRA so easily, even after seeing how easily he would go against the socialist message. However, he has privately taken a turn against Cold's leadership of the Rifle Association in a leaked memo that we have for you today. Without any more introduction, here it is:

The Memo.
